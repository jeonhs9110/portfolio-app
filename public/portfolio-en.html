Featured Project 05

EPL Match Prediction AI Hyunsik Jeon (Personal Use)

Python · TensorFlow · Reinforcement Learning · Monte Carlo · XGBoost · LightGBM · FastAPI · Pandas
A high-precision forecasting system analyzing data from the EPL and other major European leagues. It maximizes predictive accuracy by ensembling deep learning models with reinforcement learning strategies.

6-Step System Architecture

  • Step 1 Data Scrapping: Automated pipeline for match data and global bookmaker odds collection.
  • Step 2 Predictive Ensembling: Triple architecture — TensorFlow DL + XGBoost + LightGBM.
  • Step 3 Confidence Optimization: Reinforcement Learning (RL) agent for tuning logical confidence thresholds.
  • Step 4 Probabilistic Reasoning: Monte Carlo simulation for calibrated outcome probabilities.
  • Step 5 Dynamic Tuning: Adaptive sequence length optimization based on league-specific volatility.
  • Step 6 Power Ranking: ELO-based strength metrics and GNN-based dominance analysis.
